# Studio Update: Spring 2024

## A Season of Transformation

It has been an incredible year of growth for our studio. As we move through spring 2024, we find ourselves reflecting on the remarkable journey of the past twelve months — a period defined not just by project completions and awards, but by the deepening of our creative culture and the expansion of our capabilities.

From welcoming exceptional new talent to preparing for our first major retrospective exhibition in Milan, this season marks a pivotal moment in STUDIO's evolution. We're excited to share these developments with you, our community of clients, collaborators, and design enthusiasts.

---

## Welcoming New Voices

### Expanding Our Creative Family

We have welcomed three new senior architects to the STUDIO team, each bringing distinctive expertise and fresh perspectives:

**Elena Vasquez — Senior Architect, Sustainability Lead**
Elena joins us from Foster + Partners, where she led environmental performance analysis on several Passivhaus-certified projects. With a master's degree in Sustainable Design from the Architectural Association and LEED Fellow accreditation, Elena will be spearheading our growing net-zero practice.

_"I was drawn to STUDIO because of their commitment to making sustainability integral to design rather than an add-on certification. The holistic approach here is rare and refreshing."_

**James Okonkwo — Senior Architect, Urban Design**
Formerly with OMA in Rotterdam, James brings deep experience in large-scale urban interventions and mixed-use developments. His award-winning work on the Lagos Waterfront Masterplan demonstrated how architecture can catalyze community development.

_"STUDIO's balance of conceptual rigor and built reality is exactly where I want my practice to live. I'm excited to help bridge our speculative work with transformative urban projects."_

**Sophia Laurent — Senior Architect, Cultural + Institutional**
Sophia arrives from Atelier Jean Nouvel, where she spent eight years developing museums and cultural centers across Europe and Asia. Her expertise in complex institutional projects will strengthen our expanding cultural portfolio.

_"The opportunity to lead STUDIO's first major museum project in Paris was irresistible. This team understands that cultural architecture must serve communities first, aesthetics second."_

## Milan Exhibition: "Material Dialogues"

### Our First Major Retrospective

We are thrilled to announce our first major retrospective exhibition, **"Material Dialogues: 12 Years of STUDIO Architecture,"** opening this September at the Triennale Milano during Milan Design Week.

The exhibition will span 500 square meters across the Triennale's prestigious Galleria del Design, featuring:

**Archival Materials**

- Original concept sketches from 25 key projects
- Study models (many never publicly displayed)
- Construction photographs documenting the making of our built work
- Material samples and finish explorations

**New Commissions**

- A site-specific installation exploring our signature material palette
- An immersive film documenting our design process
- Interactive digital archive allowing visitors to explore our portfolio
- Full-scale facade mock-up demonstrating the Horizon Tower kinetic system

**Public Programming**

- Daily lunchtime talks by STUDIO principals
- Weekend workshops for emerging architects
- Evening panel discussions with collaborators and clients
- Book launch of our first monograph (published by Phaidon)

### Exhibition Details

## Project Milestones

### Recently Completed

**Alpine Retreat, Switzerland** (February 2024)
Our off-grid mountain residence received its final certificate of occupancy in February after three years of design and construction. The project has been featured in Dezeen, Wallpaper\*, and Architectural Digest, with a full monograph forthcoming.

**Villa Nova, Tuscany** (March 2024)
The Tuscan hillside villa completed commissioning in March, achieving LEED Platinum certification — the first residential project in the region to earn the rating. The family moved in just before Easter and has already hosted three dinner parties in the courtyard.

**Horizon Tower, Singapore — Phase 1** (April 2024)
The first 20 floors of Horizon Tower are now fully leased, with anchor tenant a global technology firm occupying the sky lobby level. Phase 2 construction continues on schedule for December 2025 completion.

### Breaking Ground

**Kyoto Zen Pavilion — Construction Begins** (June 2024)
After four years in development, the Zen Pavilion will break ground next month. The project has secured full funding through a private foundation and is expected to complete in late 2026. We will be documenting construction through a dedicated project journal.

**Paris Museum of Contemporary Art — Design Development**
Our competition-winning scheme for a new contemporary art museum in the 13th arrondissement has moved into design development. The 12,000-square-meter project includes gallery spaces, a research library, and a rooftop sculpture terrace with Eiffel Tower views.

### In Competition

**Sydney Harbour Cultural Centre — Shortlisted**
We are honored to be among five firms shortlisted for the Sydney Harbour Cultural Centre competition. Our scheme proposes a series of pavilions that float above the water, connected by bridges and accessible by ferry. A winner will be announced in August.

## In Memoriam

We were saddened to learn of the passing of Dr. Robert "Bob" Henderson, a dear friend of the studio and structural engineer on our first five built projects. Bob passed away peacefully at his home in Marin County on April 22 at age 78. His wisdom, humor, and structural ingenuity shaped our early work, and his legacy lives on in every building we design. A memorial service will be held at the studio on June 8.
